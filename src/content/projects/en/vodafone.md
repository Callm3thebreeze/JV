## Overview

A comprehensive redesign of an e-commerce platform, rethinking the entire shopping experience from product discovery to checkout.

## The Challenge

The existing platform had a high cart abandonment rate and users struggled to find products efficiently. The navigation was confusing and the checkout process had too many steps.

## My Approach

I conducted user research, created user flows, and designed a new information architecture. The design system was built from scratch with accessibility in mind.

## Results

- 35% reduction in cart abandonment
- 50% faster product discovery
- Improved accessibility scores
