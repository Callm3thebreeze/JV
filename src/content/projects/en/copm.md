## Overview

A digital redesign for the Official College of Psychologists of Madrid, created to organize a large volume of institutional information into a clearer, more approachable experience.

## Goals

- Improve navigation for professionals, members, and citizens.
- Surface services, training, and resources with less friction.
- Convey credibility through a cleaner visual system and more structured content.

## Design and Development

The project combined UX thinking, interface design, and front-end implementation. The focus was on accessibility, content hierarchy, and responsive behavior across long-form informational pages.

## Outcome

The result is a more legible institutional site with stronger guidance, better content discoverability, and a calmer visual language aligned with the organization's public role.
